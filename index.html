🔍 Data Collection Transparency

TrendView is committed to protecting your privacy. This application is designed with privacy-first principles and collects no personal data from its users. We believe in minimal data collection and complete transparency about how our app functions.

📋 Data We Collect

We collect absolutely no data. TrendView operates entirely without collecting, storing, or transmitting any personal information or device identifiers.

Specifically, we do NOT collect:

  • No Personal Information - We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact details
  • No Device Identifiers - We do not collect IMEI, MAC address, Android ID, or any unique device identifiers
  • No Location Data - We do not track your physical location
  • No Usage Analytics - We do not track how you use the app
  • No Crash Reports - We do not collect crash logs or diagnostic information
  • No Advertising ID - We do not access or store your advertising identifier
  • No Financial Information - We do not process or store any payment details

🔑 Permission Justification

Why we need certain permissions:

Permission Purpose
INTERNET Used exclusively to download publicly available market data from the Moscow Exchange (MOEX) API. This includes stock tickers, candle data, and company logos. No personal data is transmitted.
Network State Used to check internet connectivity before attempting to download market data, ensuring a smooth user experience.

🛡️ How Your Data Is Used

Since we collect no data, there is no user data to use. The app functions entirely locally on your device:

  • Stock lists and market data are downloaded directly from public MOEX API endpoints
  • All calculations (RSI-VWAP, MFI indicators) are performed locally on your device
  • Your favorite stocks list is stored only on your device using Android's local storage
  • App preferences and settings are stored only locally on your device
  • No information ever leaves your device except for standard API calls to MOEX for market data

📤 Data Storage & Sharing

Data Storage:

  • All app data (favorites list, settings) is stored exclusively on your device
  • No cloud storage, no remote servers, no external databases
  • Market data is temporarily cached on your device for offline access and performance

Data Sharing:

  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT sell any data (as there is no data to sell)
  • We do NOT transfer data across borders
  • We do NOT use any analytics services
  • We do NOT use any advertising networks

🗑️ Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

Complete data removal is simple:
  • Uninstall the app - This immediately and permanently removes all app data (favorites, settings, cached market data) from your device
  • Clear app data - You can also go to Android Settings → Apps → TrendView → Storage → Clear Data to remove all app information

No additional action is required, as we maintain no servers with your information.

👤 User Rights

As we collect no personal data, most traditional privacy rights (access, correction, deletion of personal data) are inherently satisfied. However, you always have the right to:

  • Uninstall the app at any time, removing all local data
  • Contact us with any privacy questions or concerns
